# Essence Studio Legal Boundary

Essence Studio is an original, self-owned visual design product. It can compete with browser-based design tools, but it must not copy their protected identity or proprietary content.

## Product Rules

- Use the production-facing name `Essence Studio`.
- Keep the UI, templates, vector packs, presets, and starter content original to this codebase.
- Do not use Canva logos, trade dress, proprietary templates, paid media, screenshots, or brand copy as shipped product assets.
- Use only user uploads, original local assets, or free/open assets with source and license metadata.
- Keep AI features out of scope until the non-AI editor remains strong on its own.

## Current Implementation

- The app stores source, author, license, and provider metadata for imported Wikimedia Commons stock assets.
- Built-in templates, style presets, vector packs, and mockup frames are local/original product assets.
- User-facing app, auth, email, metadata, and desktop shell branding use `Essence Studio`.
- Internal repo/database history may still reference the original Essence Canva project codename.
